Hadoop Tutorial 1

After an exhausting search for Hadoop tutorials, I decided to design my own one. There are several reasons here. First, most online courses are not free and cost too much for a poor student like me. Meanwhile, there are few free ones and they are a little bit outdated. But the good news is that Hadoop is an open source project, so most resource can be found on the internet. The only tough thing is that how to find the right one that can help your learning in a natural, efficient and effective way. That is my goal at the beginning and also what this tutorial is for. I will share my learning process and materials here. Importantly, as a new baby to Hadoop, I can share the solutions to the problems that are common for other beginners. We all want to save time, otherwise you may not need to use Hadoop. Yeah, Hadoop saves your life.

In this article, I will deal with three topics:

  1. What problems does Hadoop solve and how it solves them
  2. Hadoop distributed file system basics and architecture
  3. Setting up pseudo-distributed file system in your computer

1. Big data and Hadoop

Alright, I use big data to attract your eyeballs even though it is not our topic. But that is the background of Hadoop. We will begin with the Yahoo! Hadoop tutorial. Module 1 talk about the scope of problems applicable to Hadoop and how Hadoop addresses these problems. It gives us a big picture. And I like big pictures since they make our learning more efficient.
